# Business Valuation Utility Documentation

## Overview
The Business Valuation Utility leverages machine learning to provide insights into business performance and optimal pricing strategies.

## Component Architecture

### MarketTrendAnalyzer
**Purpose**: Analyzes market trends using real-time data.
- **Inputs**: Market-related data (e.g., competitor prices, economic indicators)
- **Outputs**: Trends and forecasts

### RiskAssessor
**Purpose**: Assesses business risks based on financial health
